Must-Watch Environmental Movies for Earth Day

The best environmental films don't lecture. They show you what's at stake in a way the news rarely does, and they stay with you for weeks.

Chasing Coral

Photographers and scientists set out to document coral bleaching—a sign of mass coral death—in real time. It took them years, and the footage they captured is devastating and beautiful in equal measure. Nearly a decade later, the film feels even more urgent than it did on day one.

The Location: Lahaina, HI

Instaworthy Accommodations:

Sitting on 54 acres along Maui's protected northwest shore, The Ritz-Carlton Maui, Kapalua is surrounded by 22,000 acres of open land—including the Honokahua Preservation Site, a sacred Hawaiian landmark directly adjacent.
